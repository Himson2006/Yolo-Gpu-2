How to Use the Search

Enter criteria into one or more fields below and click "Search" to find matching videos. Leave all fields blank to see no results initially.

  • Available Classes:
    'Common_Raccoon', 'Mule_Deer', 'White-Tailed_Deer, 'Nine-Banded_Armadillo', 'Rabbit_Hare', 'Vulture'
  • Class name: Enter a single class or multiple classes separated by commas (e.g., Deer, Squirrel).
  • Match Type: "Match Any" (OR) finds videos with any listed species. "Match All" (AND) requires all listed species to be present.
  • Min Video Duration: Find videos with a duration greater than or equal to the specified seconds.
  • Date Range: Specify a start and end date to filter videos by when they were recorded.
  • Device ID: Filter by the device that recorded the video
  • Time of Day: Choose "Day" (6am - 6pm) or "Night" (6pm - 6am) to filter videos by the time they were recorded.
